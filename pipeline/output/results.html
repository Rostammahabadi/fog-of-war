Methodology: Based on the paper "When AI Navigates the Fog of War". Each model receives temporally-gated intelligence briefings and must forecast escalating events across 11 nodes. 1-MAE (Calibration Consistency) is the paper's primary metric: 1 minus mean absolute error between predicted probability and binary outcome (higher = better). Brier Score measures calibration (lower = better). Accuracy is binary prediction correctness. Probabilities extracted via LLM (Gemini Flash) with qualitative language mapping. Ground truth from 42 verifiable binary questions. Built on a corpus of 1,861 real news articles from 12 international outlets, temporally gated per node.
